Details of the workshop :

The fees for the workshop is Rs.1000 per head for Students of IIITDM and Rs.1200 per head for the students from other colleges.

The following items are provided to every participant:

  • A Software CD
  • Authorized certification from SenseFil Technologies
  • Email support to all the participants (in case of some queries)

By the end of the day , we are taught on the following aspects

  • How to convert a normal Laptop or Desktop Screen into multi-touch Screen i.e. we will be able to give input through the screen of the laptop/desktop.
  • The image thrown by projector on any surface will be converted into multi-touch surface.
  • We will be able to make our own multi-touch pad.
  • We will be able to control our laptop with hand gesture movement through the device Microsoft KINECT.
  • Controlling mouse(cursor) with voice.
  • Can Play Virtual Drum
  • Can make a Wireless Measuring Tape
  • Can capture 3-D Image through Video
  • Can create our own Augmented world and play with it
